OUR STORY

Oriowo Folarin's Hub

From a young age, I Folarin was captivated by computers. While my friends played outside, I spent hours disassembling and reassembling my father's old PC, eager to understand how it worked. By 12, I started coding simple websites and games, and in high school, I started a tech club where I and some of my seniors’ mentored others in programming and robotics Determined to turn my passion into something bigger, I pursued a degree in Data science at SBS Vietnam, and I’m currently working on launching my own tech hub which is Folarin Tech Hub—a space for young people to learn, create, and innovate. This hub will offer workshops, coding bootcamps, and hands-on projects that will help aspiring tech enthusiasts turn their ideas into reality. Folarin’s dream of providing a platform for creativity and learning will become a reality, and soon, the tech hub will become a thriving community where future tech leaders can come to grow, collaborate, and make an impact in the world.
